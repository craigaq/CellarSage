Our Technology

A proprietary algorithm built for the Australian market.

Generic recommendation engines treat all wine markets the same. We built ours from the ground up for Australia — accounting for regional distribution, local merchant stock, and the unique character of Australian wine regions.

Every recommendation comes with a confidence score, ranked in real time against what's actually available near you.

  • Processes location, style, price, and occasion simultaneously
  • Cross-references real-time merchant availability in your area
  • Learns regional trends unique to Australian wine culture
  • Returns ranked results with transparency on match reasoning
  • Your palate data is never sold to third parties
