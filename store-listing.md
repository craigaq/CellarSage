## Full description (≤4000 chars; current ~1,900)
```
Cellar Sage is your personal sommelier AND cicerone — it learns how you like to drink, then matches you to wine and beer you can actually buy right now in Australia.

No jargon. No guessing. Just answer a few quick questions about your taste, and the Cellar Fox does the rest.

— HOW IT WORKS —
Take the 2-minute palate quiz. For wine, dial in acidity, body, tannin and flavour. For beer, it's bitterness, body, fizz and aroma. Tell us what you're eating, and whether you want your drink to harmonise with the dish or contrast it.

— YOUR PICKS, RANKED —
Cellar Sage returns tiered recommendations, from your own backyard to the wider world:
• Local Hero — the best drop from your own state
• The Interstater — a standout from across Australia
• The Internationalist — a global icon worth the trip
Every pick comes with a match score, tasting notes, price, and a direct link to buy.

— NOW WITH BEER —
The same palate-match smarts, built for beer the cicerone way. Discover your top beer styles, see Untappd ratings, and spot the ones that are "Highly Rated for their style." Per-drink pricing lets you compare a single can to a full carton fairly.

— RATINGS YOU TRUST —
Vivino community ratings and Wine Enthusiast critic scores for wine. Untappd ratings for beer. Quality signals on every recommendation.

— FOOD PAIRING —
Tell us the dish — from steak to spicy, charcuterie to barbecue — and Cellar Sage explains why each match works, in plain language.

— BUY IN ONE TAP —
Recommendations link straight through to major Australian retailers. Find it, tap it, done.

Cellar Sage promotes responsible alcohol consumption. You must be of legal drinking age to use this app. Please drink responsibly.

Made in South Australia. 🦊
```
